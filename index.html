British born and Brazilian raised, Gabriel Graham is a international specialist in communications, athleticism, and computer science.

As a teacher of over ten years and leader of large teams for five, his tenure in public speaking and personal development is well established in the lives of those he has impacted.

A two time world champion in martial arts and diversely trained sparring competitor, Gabriel Graham is no stranger to a challenge and exemplifies determination in every pursuit.

In his newest journey, he has chosen to grow in software development and coding, specializing in multiple computing languages including HTML and JavaScript. In his free time, he can be found biking, spending time with his wife and five pets, or traveling the world.
